The Problem

The people building culture are being forced out by the people who don't care about it.

Independent promoters, music collectives, and underground venues run the most culturally significant events in the world — and they do it on WhatsApp threads, Google Sheets, and gut instinct.

Meanwhile, commercial operators with capital and infrastructure are swallowing the scene. Not because they're better — because they have better tools.

💸
Financial chaos
Bar splits, artist payments, expense tracking — all manual. Collectives bleed money they don't know they're losing.
📋
No operational layer
Ticketing, guest lists, lineups, artist contracts — stitched together from 6 different apps with no single source of truth.
📣
Marketing left to chance
Flyers on Instagram and word of mouth. No data on who shows up, what works, or how to grow the audience consistently.
The Solution

One agentic OS. Everything the scene needs to operate.

Nocturn is the vertical SaaS platform for independent music culture — the same transformation Toast brought to restaurants, Mindbody brought to fitness. Nocturn brings it to the operators who make music culture alive.

🎟️
Events & Ticketing
Create events, manage guest lists, sell tickets, track door revenue — all from one place. No Eventbrite cut, no Posh dependency.
💰
Finance & Payouts
Automatic bar split calculations, artist payments, expense tracking. Know exactly what you made on every night.
🤖
AI Agents
Finance agent, marketing agent, growth agent, operations agent — autonomous workflows that run the business so operators can focus on the music.
📊
Marketing & Growth
Audience analytics, campaign tools, and data on who shows up. Turn one-time attendees into loyal community members.
We're building this because music culture deserves to stay independent. The tools have always existed for commercial operators. It's time they existed for the people who actually love the music.

The Bigger Picture

We're not building a ticketing app.
We're building the financial infrastructure for music culture.

The vertical OS is Phase 1. But the long-term vision is significantly larger — a three-sided platform that becomes the economic backbone of the entire independent music ecosystem.

Phase 1 · Now
The OS
Vertical SaaS for operators. Replace the spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads, and duct-taped tools with one agentic platform.
Phase 2 · 2026
The Marketplace
Three-sided network: operators attract fans, fans attract artists, artists attract operators. Each side makes the others more valuable.
Phase 3 · 2027
Embedded Fintech
Payments, float management, artist revenue splits, instant payouts. Nocturn becomes the bank account for the scene — Toast's path to $16B.
Phase 4 · 2028+
The Data Layer
Audience intelligence, scene analytics, music discovery infrastructure. The Spotify Wrapped for culture — and the ad platform that powers it.
🔄
Network effects compound
Every operator that joins makes the fan discovery better. Every fan on the platform makes the artist booking easier. Every artist on the roster makes operators want in. The moat deepens with every user.
💳
Fintech is the real margin
SaaS fees are the wedge. Payment processing, cash advances, and embedded financial products are where vertical SaaS companies actually win — see Toast, Mindbody, Shopify. The music scene moves billions annually.
🌍
Culture is global
Underground music culture isn't a Toronto problem or a NYC problem — it's Berlin, London, São Paulo, Nairobi. The playbook that works in one city can expand city by city, culture by culture. The TAM grows with us.
